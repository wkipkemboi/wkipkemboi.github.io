About Me

A decade of geospatial impact

I am a GIS and Earth Observation professional with over a decade of experience transforming complex geospatial data into actionable insights. My work spans enterprise GIS, remote sensing, spatial analysis, and data visualisation — with a focus on sustainable development and environmental management.

I have deep expertise in ArcGIS Enterprise deployment, web-based GIS development with Django and GeoDjango, and managing spatial databases with PostgreSQL/PostGIS. I leverage Google Earth Engine for remote sensing analysis and work across ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, and ERDAS Imagine.

Python powers my GeoAI projects. With a strong background in research and consultancy, I have authored and co-authored multiple peer-reviewed publications and am always open to opportunities that harness geospatial technology for meaningful change.
